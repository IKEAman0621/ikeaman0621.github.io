Background

History and purpose

Super metroid is a game developed and released by nintendo in 1994. The game follows bounty hunter Samus Aran on a mission to rid the planet Zebes from the space pirate invasion, aswell as the sentient supercomputer mother brain. To do this the player is expected to collect items, defeat bosses, and explore the underground caverns of Zebes. Culminating in defeating mother brain and escaping as the planet explodes behind you. Because of severall oversights during development however, the game is able to be played however the player wants, with the only limiting factor being player skill. This allows for an experience with the game that is neverendingly complex and infinitely improvable. Because of this freedom the game has garnered a large speedrunning community, being the 19th most ran game on speedrun.com

Technical information

Some important technical information is that the game runs at 60 frames per second, making one frame about 0.016 seconds.

Target audience / goal

This website acts as a small guide to the easiest tricks and optimisations, giving the user a glimpse into the complex world of super metroid speedrunning. This ISN'T a guide to the game, rather to a few speedrunning tricks, therefore basic movement, the route, jumping and shooting won't be explained. This site is aimed towards the people who want to take their Super Metroid gameplay to new heights, either playing for speed, hitting impressive tricks or going for low percent runs. All up to you.
